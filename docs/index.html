About This Project

The Email Template Bookshelf is a curated collection of HTML email templates designed for various clients and use cases. This repository serves as both a personal archive and a resource for client demonstrations.

All templates are responsive, tested across major email clients, and follow accessibility best practices to ensure the widest possible reach for your email campaigns.

Templates marked as "CMG Demo" are also available for demonstration at College Marketing Group as starting points for our clients.
